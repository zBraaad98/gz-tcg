About GZ-TCG

GZ-TCG is a UK-based sole trader business specialising in the sourcing, grading, and sale of high-quality trading card singles. We focus on collectible cards that demonstrate strong condition and long-term demand, with an emphasis on professional third-party grading.

All graded cards available through GZ-TCG are professionally authenticated and encapsulated by recognised independent grading companies, including PSA, Beckett (BGS), ACE Grading, and CGC. GZ-TCG does not perform grading itself; grading reports and grades are issued by the relevant grading partner.

Our graded cards are securely shipped and sold through trusted online platforms, with transparent photos, accurate condition descriptions, and established seller credentials.

Our Approach

  • Selected based on condition, rarity, and grading potential
  • Submitted to trusted third-party graders
  • Listed with clear, honest descriptions
  • Shipped securely with tracking and protection

Why GZ-TCG

With established positive feedback from online selling platforms, we aim to deliver a professional and trustworthy experience for buyers looking for graded trading cards. Whether you are a collector or reseller, our listings reflect careful selection and accurate presentation.
